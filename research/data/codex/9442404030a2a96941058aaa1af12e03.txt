Hampton Roads residents are gearing up for another round of peaceful demonstrations in Chesapeake for Monday. Here is the timeline of events in Hampton Roads Monday: 8:20 p.m. — Demonstrators are still gathered at Chesapeake City Hall after at least 1,000 people went on a 2.5-mile march for racial justice.
