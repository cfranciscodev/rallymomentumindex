Protesters march for Michael Brown and Eric Garner in Paris News by: Staff Writer Posted: Dec 7, 2014 / 09:50 AM CST / Updated: Dec 7, 2014 / 09:50 AM CST This is an archived article and the information in the article may be outdated. Please look at the time stamp on the story to see when it was last updated. Please enable Javascript to watch this video PARIS, FRANCE - American protests have gone international. After recent grand jury decisions to not indict police officers in New York and Ferguson for the deaths of Eric Garner and Michael Brown. Demonstrators in Paris took to the Eiffel tower Saturday to protest against police brutality. French teenagers, watched and shot this video of the demonstration. People from all over the world were holding signs in support of the victims.
